How Do We Use Cookies?

A cookie is a small file that is placed on your computer’s hard drive with your permission. Cookies are used to collect information on your browsing preferences so that web applications can tailor their operations to meet your needs, likes, and dislikes.

Maple Web Works uses an implied consent policy. This means that we use cookies and assume that you are OK with this. If however, you are not, you must delete our cookies after visiting our site or use an anonymous browsing session when visiting our site.
